Crayon Shin-chan, also known as Shin Chan, is a Japanese manga and anime series written and illustrated by Yoshito Usui.
The story follows the adventures of a five-year-old boy named Shinnosuke “Shin” Nohara, who is often called Shin-chan by his family. Set in the city of Kasukabe in Saitama Prefecture, within the Greater Tokyo Area of Japan, the series takes us through the humorous escapades of Shin-chan, his parents, baby sister, dog, neighbors, and best friends
