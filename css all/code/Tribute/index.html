A.P.J ABDUL KALAM

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalm (15 October 1931 - 27 July 2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamilnadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. He spent the next four decades as ascientist administrator, mainly at the Defence Research and Development Organisation(DRDO) and Indian Space Research Organisation(ISRO).
